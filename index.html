City Social Welfare and Development Office Unit 9 Baguio City

The City Social Welfare and Development Office (CSWDO) is the local social welfare arm of the City Government of Baguio
mandated to provide basic social welfare programs and services to its disadvantaged citizenry.

Services We Offer

We from the Unit 9 offer services whether financial or psycological help for the citizens of Irisan, Baguio City.

Counseling

For issues and problems that need to be fixed, we offer our service to help resolve the problem.

Financial

We from the CSWDO Unit 9 offers services or to be more precise, offer help in way of scholarships and such.

Aid

In times of crisis such as this pandemic, we provide help that is beneficial to every families.

Vision, Mission, Core Values

Vision

We envision that the disadvantages , oppressed the poor and the powerless exploited davaoeños to become empowered productive and self reliant.
